The POPCO Offer

  • Get an assorted box of seasonal fruit and vegetables for only $55.
  • Boxes are available for collection from the Pacific Palms Community Centre, The Lakes Way, Elizabeth Beach, between 5.30-6pm every fortnight.
  • Box collection dates for start of 2018 are: 11 Jan; 25 Jan; 8 Feb; 22 Feb; 8 Mar; 22 Mar; 5 Apr; 19 Apr.
  • Each box will usually contains some regulars like lettuce, carrots, zucchini, potatoes, apples and bananas along with a good portion of seasonal favourites.
  • We estimate that our box is up to 40% cheaper than standard retail prices because we benefit from the same wholesale prices that shops do.

We Are Locals Who Care
