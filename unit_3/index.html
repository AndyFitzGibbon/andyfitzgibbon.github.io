Organic Fruit and Vegetable Boxes

Affordable, fresh and pesticide free food for happy, healthy families

Click Here to Order Your Box Now for Collection at Pacific Palms NSW

POPCO makes it easy for you to find affordable organic produce for your home. Our boxes are a great way to enjoy chemical free nutritious meals, save money off your grocery bill and support local and home grown organic producers.

  • Get an assorted box of seasonal fruit and vegetables for only $55 every fortnight on Thursdays.
  • Boxes are collected from the Pacific Palms Community Centre, The Lakes Way, Elizabeth Beach.
  • Each box will usually contains some regulars like lettuce, carrots, zucchini, potatoes, apples, bananas etc along with a good portion of seasonal favourites.
  • We estimate that our box is up to 40% cheaper than standard retail prices because we benefit from the same wholesale prices that shops do.
