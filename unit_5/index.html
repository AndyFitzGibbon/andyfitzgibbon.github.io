Organic Fruit and Vegetable Boxes

Affordable, fresh and pesticide-free food for healthy families

What We Offer

  • Get an assorted box of seasonal fruit and vegetables for only $55 every fortnight on Thursdays.
  • Boxes are collected from the Pacific Palms Community Centre, The Lakes Way, Elizabeth Beach.
  • Box collection dates for 2017 are as follows: 16 Nov; 30 Nov; 14 Dec; 28 Dec.
  • Each box will usually contains some regulars like lettuce, carrots, zucchini, potatoes, apples and bananas along with a good portion of seasonal favourites.
  • We estimate that our box is up to 40% cheaper than standard retail prices because we benefit from the same wholesale prices that shops do.
  • Email popco.books@gmail.com or call 0400 000 000 to place your order for the next fresh fruit and vegetable box or for more information.
